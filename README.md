Retail Sales Performance Analysis (Tableau)
Project Overview
This project is a comprehensive analysis of retail sales and profitability from 2023 to 2025. Using Tableau, I transformed raw sales data into an interactive dashboard that identifies key growth drivers, regional dominance, and product-level profit margins.

Final Dashboard
Data Insights
Based on the analysis of the dataset:

Total Revenue: The business generated $350,500 in total sales over the tracked period.

Profitability Leader: The Electronics category maintains the highest profit margins, even when sales volume fluctuates.

Regional Market Share: The East and West regions are the primary revenue drivers, as shown in the Treemap analysis.

Growth Trends: Significant sales spikes were observed in 2024, particularly within the Phones and Chairs product lines.

Visual Breakdown
1. Sales Over Time & Product Performance

This multi-line chart tracks how different products performed across a three-year span. It highlights seasonality and peak sales periods.

2. Profit vs. Margin Analysis (Scatter Plot)

Used to identify "Stars" (High Sales/High Profit) and "Underperformers." Electronics clearly outpaces Furniture in margin efficiency.

3. Regional Distribution (Treemap)

A proportional view of the four major regions (East, West, North, South) to quickly identify geographical strengths.

4. Segment & Category Split

Detailed breakdowns of the customer base (Consumer, Corporate, Home Office) and primary product categories.

Technical Features
Global Interactivity: Implemented Year, Region, and Category filters to allow for deep-dive analysis.

Data Cleaning: Handled multi-year date formats and categorized product hierarchies for clean visualization.

UI/UX Design: Focused on a "Control Panel" layout for intuitive navigation.

Tools Used
Tableau Desktop: Data visualization and dashboard design.

Microsoft Excel: Initial data storage and cleaning.
